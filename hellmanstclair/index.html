About us
—

Hellman St Clair is founded by experienced media producers with backgrounds in film production, live entertainment, advertising and content production. The consultancy works internationally and domestically for clients such as IKEA, Octapharma, Zara Larsson, MTV Unplugged, VISA, Specsavers, Nintendo, Dramaten, the BBC, Channel 4, DR and SVT.

Hellman St Clair works alongside a global network of skilled talent - artists, directors, creators, influencers, technologists, designers, thinkers and doers - to create bespoke strategy and content across e-commerce, video, stills, animation, and emerging media formats.


Our approach
—

We approach projects from a broad, multi-disciplinary perspective, encouraging people to think critically and creatively to find the strongest ideas and land the best solutions. We structure engagements in sprints to tackle new problems for our clients and during the creation of content productions.

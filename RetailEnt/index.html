1. Content creation and installation of screens
—

Our background in content creation in large scaled live productions for brands and broadcasters has shaped our thought-process. Where we're from, screens are used as building blocks of larger environments. They are props in a scenery, used to enhance the story we are about to tell. By putting some thought into the shape of a screen and the atmosphere surrounding it, you’re pretty much guaranteed to get eye-popping results. While the content of a screen is important, the context of where it sits is most often overlooked.

Our content creators and designers are helping artists, brands and broadcasters in the fields of interior design, set design and installations.
